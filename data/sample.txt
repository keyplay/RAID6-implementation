NTU 30th anniversary marks a history of sparking innovative ideas and addressing global challenges. Since its inauguration in 1991, NTU has been home to an exceptional community of faculty, students and staff who have helped propel the university to excellence. Join us in celebrating this occasion by contributing your best memories of NTU to the NTU Digital Time Capsule. To be opened in 2041, this capsule will be a repository of the most significant achievements of a dynamic young university.
